Who am I

I am Hongyu Chen, an undergraduate student in Xi'an Jiaotong University majoring in biology.


Well, everything could be attractive to me...... I have spent two years with synthetic biology and biophysics (more spesifically, Molecular Dynamics simulation), with the experience of iGEM competition and scientific research.


That's not the whole story. Immunology, oncology and neuroscience are also charming and have been the areas I want to explore. The exploration and treatment of cancer, autoimmune diseases and neurological diseases are my expectations for my future endeavors. So, everything is interesting enough, while travelling along the more disciplines always brings me the more exciting ideas and passions.


"To know something about everything; to know everything about something."
